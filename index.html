Chase Rogers SWOT Analysis

Strengths

• I’m good at staying focused, once I decide to commit to something, I’ll get it done.

• I’m good at learning, I tend to retain what I have learned and am usually able to grasp concepts with “little” difficulty.

Weaknesses

• I struggle with social interaction, I’m not a conversationalist/open person

• I have a hard time articulating/communicating my thoughts and ideas

• I procrastinate a lot when at home/struggle with motivating outside of the workplace

Opportunities

Threats

• I am new and unexperienced in the coding field.

• While this is a large job market, more and more people are stepping into it through various academies and courses, making entry positions a coveted commodity.
